Privacy Policy for InstagramViewer

Last updated: January 20, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how InstagramViewer ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application that integrates with Instagram's services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Instagram

When you connect your Instagram account to InstagramViewer, we may access and collect:

  • Basic profile information (username, profile picture)
  • Media content (photos and videos) that you choose to share
  • Media metadata (captions, timestamps, location data if available)
  • Account insights and analytics data (if applicable)

2.2 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Feedback and support communications
  • Settings and preferences within the app

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser)
  • Usage data and app performance metrics
  • IP address and general location information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the InstagramViewer service
  • Display your Instagram content as requested
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure app security and prevent misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • Instagram/Meta: As required by Instagram's API terms and policies
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in app operations (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed
  • We retain data only as long as necessary for service provision

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdrawal: Revoke consent and disconnect your Instagram account at any time

7. Instagram Integration

This app uses Instagram's API and is subject to Instagram's Terms of Use and Data Policy. By using InstagramViewer, you also agree to Instagram's terms. We comply with Instagram's Platform Policy and API Terms of Service.

8. Children's Privacy

InstagramViewer is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. International Users

If you are accessing InstagramViewer from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing notice through the app (for significant changes)

11. Third-Party Services

InstagramViewer may integrate with third-party services for analytics, hosting, or other functionality. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.
